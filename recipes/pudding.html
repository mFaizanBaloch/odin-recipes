Pudding Recipe

Description

This pudding recipe begins with heating milk until it simmers. A mixture of sugar, cornstarch, and salt is gradually added and cooked until thickened. Vanilla extract is stirred in before chilling the pudding until set.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, heat 2 cups of whole milk over medium heat until it just begins to simmer.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together 1/2 cup granulated sugar, 1/4 cup cornstarch, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Gradually whisk the dry mixture into the simmering milk. Stir constantly until the mixture thickens, about 5 minutes.
  4. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract until well combined.
  5. Pour the pudding into individual serving dishes. Let it cool at room temperature for about 15 minutes, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours until chilled and set.
  6. Serve the chilled pudding plain or with toppings of your choice, such as whipped cream, fruit, or chocolate shavings.
